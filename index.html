About K & S Optical

Welcome to K & S Optical, your premier destination for quality eyewear in Bahadurgarh, Haryana. Established and managed by Krishan Saini, MIE-Part 2, we have been serving the community with exceptional optical products and services for years.

Our extensive collection includes premium sunglasses, spectacle frames, and fashion optical wear from renowned brands. We pride ourselves on offering personalized service, expert advice, and the latest trends in eyewear fashion.

At K & S Optical, we understand that your vision is precious, which is why we combine style with functionality to provide you with eyewear that not only looks great but also protects and enhances your vision.
